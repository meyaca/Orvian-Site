What we automate

AI agents built around
your actual workflows

We do not sell software subscriptions or generic chatbots. We build custom AI agents that integrate into your existing tools and processes — and produce measurable output from the first week.

RFI Response Agent
Reads your drawing set and project specifications. Drafts responses to incoming requests for information by pulling the relevant clauses and references. Routes complex requests to the appropriate discipline lead. Logs everything automatically to your project management system.
9 days down to same day
Specification Compliance Checker
Compares drawing sets against the National Building Code, International Building Code, or project-specific specifications. Returns a structured discrepancy report ranked by risk level — in 48 hours instead of three weeks, before issues reach the field.
3 weeks down to 48 hours
Proposal and Scope Writer
Generates first drafts using your past project wins, established fee structures, and existing boilerplate language. Your team reviews and refines a near-complete document rather than writing from a blank page every time.
80 hours down to under 10
Project Status Reporter
Connects to your project management software, timesheets, and email. Automatically generates weekly owner updates and internal status reports — structured, accurate, and ready to send every Monday morning without manual assembly.
6 hours down to 20 minutes
Submittal Tracking Agent
Logs incoming submittals, assigns reviewers, sends automated reminders for overdue responses, and maintains a complete audit trail. Removes the spreadsheet-and-email coordination loop that delays review cycles on every project.
Zero manual administration

Data and privacy

Your documents remain
completely confidential

Confidentiality is non-negotiable in engineering. Here is exactly how we handle your documents and data at every stage.

API

Enterprise API — not consumer AI

We use commercial API endpoints operating under enterprise data agreements — not consumer AI products. Your inputs are never used to train any AI model, and are subject to automatic deletion policies. Full stop.

CA

Canadian data residency available

For firms requiring data to remain within Canadian jurisdiction, we deploy through AWS infrastructure hosted in Canadian regions. Your documents never leave the country.

NDA

NDA before any document is shared

We sign a mutual non-disclosure agreement and a data processing agreement before receiving any client files. You retain full ownership of all outputs. We keep nothing after the engagement ends.

OPS

Deploy inside your own infrastructure

For clients with strict information technology requirements, we build and deploy the entire solution inside your existing cloud environment using your own credentials. Your data never enters our systems at all.
